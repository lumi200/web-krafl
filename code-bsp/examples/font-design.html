Font design

Why font design matters and what to do?

In this design, the text dynamically adjusts its size for a seamless experience on various screen sizes. Having a bigger size on desktop devices and smaller size on mobile devices, makes the text easier to read. The header uses a unique display font to stand out and feel unique. It also scales to 3em on bigger screens, while the body text employs a sans-serif font and scales to 1.5em on bigger screens for easy reading. The signature is presented in a handwritten font, adding a personal touch. Thoughtful font choices and responsive design make the text visually appealing and well-suited for diverse devices and user preferences.
